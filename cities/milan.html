About Milan

Milan is one of Italy’s most cosmopolitan cities, widely known as a global capital of fashion and design. It uniquely blends rich history with modern lifestyle, featuring iconic landmarks such as the impressive Milan Cathedral and the elegant Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II. At the same time, lively areas like the Navigli canals offer a vibrant atmosphere full of cafés and restaurants, making Milan a destination that perfectly combines culture, art, and contemporary living.

Travel Note

Walking Through Milan

Walking through Milan is an experience that blends elegance, history, and everyday life. From the grand square of the Milan Cathedral to the glass-covered arcades of the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, every step reveals a mix of architectural beauty and vibrant city energy. As you wander into charming neighborhoods like Brera or along the canals of Navigli, the atmosphere becomes more relaxed, filled with cafés, art, and local life, making a simple walk feel like a journey through both past and present.
